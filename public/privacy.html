01 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use our platform.

Information you provide:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and password when you register via Firebase Authentication
  • Profile Information: Country, timezone, trading preferences, and bio that you optionally add
  • Communication: Messages you send us through our contact form

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Local Storage: Paper trading data stored locally in your browser (not on our servers)

InvestySignals uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

📢 Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to InvestySignals and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

05 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

06 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • 🔐 HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • 🔑 Passwords hashed via Firebase Authentication (never stored in plaintext)
  • 🛡️ MongoDB data stored on secure, access-controlled servers
  • 📱 Paper trading data stored locally in your browser — we never have access to it
  • 🔒 JWT tokens expire after 12 hours for admin sessions
⚠️ While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

07 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Opt-out: Opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ad Settings

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@investysignals.store

08 Children's Privacy

InvestySignals is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information.

Cryptocurrency trading involves significant financial risk and is not appropriate for minors.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting a notice on our platform dashboard
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Continued use of InvestySignals after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
