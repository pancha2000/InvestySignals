Frequently Asked Questions

Are the signals free? ▼
Yes! All signals and analysis tools on InvestySignals are completely free. We're supported by Google AdSense advertising. You'll never be asked for payment to access signals.
Are these signals financial advice? ▼
No. InvestySignals provides educational market analysis only. We are not a licensed financial advisor. Always do your own research and never trade with money you can't afford to lose.
How are signals generated? ▼
Our 8-factor confluence engine analyzes RSI, EMA, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Stochastic RSI, ADX, OBV, and Volume simultaneously on 1H Binance Futures data. Only high-confluence setups are published.
What exchange do signals work on? ▼
Signals are based on Binance Futures data (USDT-margined perpetuals). They can be used as reference for other exchanges but are optimized for Binance Futures.
How do I delete my account? ▼
Go to Profile → Security → Danger Zone to clear your data. For full account deletion, email us at privacy@investysignals.store with your request.
Can I use signals commercially or resell them? ▼
No. Signals and content are for personal educational use only. Redistribution or commercial use without written permission is prohibited under our Terms of Service.
