⚙️ Step 2 — 16 Indicators Computed
IndicatorParameters & CalculationMax Score (per side)Signal
RSIPeriod 14. Wilder smoothing on 1H closes. Formula: 100 − 100/(1 + AvgGain/AvgLoss)+20≤25 extreme OS (+20 LONG) / ≥75 extreme OB (+20 SHORT). Graduated.
EMA StackEMA20, EMA50, EMA200 on 1H closes. Exponential: k=2/(n+1)+15EMA20>EMA50>EMA200 + price above all = Perfect Bull (+15 LONG). Each partial alignment gives partial score.
MACDFast 12, Slow 26, Signal 9. Histogram = MACD − Signal+10Bullish histogram + growing = +10 LONG. Weakening = +6.
Bollinger BandsPeriod 20, 2.0 std dev. Position = (price−lower)/(upper−lower)+8Position ≤10% (near lower band) = +8 LONG. ≥90% = +8 SHORT. Squeeze (width <4%) adds +3 both.
Stoch RSIRSI period 14, Stoch 14, K=3, D=3. K and D are smoothed+8K<20 oversold = +8 LONG. K/D bullish crossover = +5.
ATRPeriod 14. True range smoothed. Used for SL sizing ONLY — no score contribution—SL = max(swing_low − 0.2×ATR, entry − 2.5×ATR). Wider for risk entries (3×).
ADXPeriod 14. DX = |+DI − −DI| / (+DI + −DI). ADX = smoothed DX+8ADX>50 strong, trending direction +8. 25–50 moderate +5. <18 = risk flag (weak trend).
OBVCumulative: +volume on up closes, −volume on down. Compare 5-bar avg vs 5-bar prior avg+8Accumulation (rising) = +8 LONG. Distribution = +8 SHORT.
VWAP 24hSum(typical_price × volume) / Sum(volume) over last 24 candles+12Price >2% above VWAP = +12 LONG. >0% = +7. Below = adds to SHORT score.
SupertrendPeriod 10, Multiplier 3. ATR-based upper/lower bands. Direction flips on close outside band+12Bullish = +12 LONG. Counter-trend = risk flag.
4H TrendEMA20/50 on 4H + price position + 4H RSI + 10-bar HH/HL structure. Score −4 to +5+20Bull bias = +20 LONG (highest weight). Opposing = risk flag.
BOS / CHoCHDetects swing highs/lows over last 35 bars. Bullish BOS = close above recent swing high+10Bullish BOS = +10 LONG. CHoCH (reversal structure) = +5 both.
FVGFair Value Gap: 3-candle imbalance. Gap[i] = close[i] > high[i-2] (bullish)+8Bullish FVG present = +8 LONG. Bearish = +8 SHORT.
RSI DivergenceCompare swing lows/highs of price vs RSI over last 30 bars. Regular = price LL + RSI HL+15 regular / +10 hiddenRegular bull = +15 LONG. Hidden bull = +10 (continuation signal).
Funding RateBinance perpetual funding. Paid every 8h. Negative = shorts pay longs+10Funding <−0.02% = +10 LONG (shorts under pressure). >+0.05% = +10 SHORT. >0.25% = risk flag.
Open InterestCompare current OI vs OI from 12 5-minute periods ago+3Rising OI = +3 both (confirms move). Falling = −3 both (positions closing).
🎯 Step 3 — Direction & Score Decision
Each indicator adds to a LONG_score or SHORT_score independently. At the end:
MAX_POSSIBLE = 185 points
confidence = min(100, round(score / MAX_POSSIBLE × 100))
direction = LONG if long_score ≥ short_score, else SHORT
conf = whichever direction's score is higher
Score thresholds:
85–100
Grade S — Excellent
70–84
Grade A — High
50–69
Grade B — Good
<50
Grade C — Risky
📍 Step 4 — Entry Type & Levels
Entry Type Logic
TypeConditionsWhat it means
MARKETConf ≥75% + volume surge + RSI ≤40 (LONG) or ≥60 (SHORT)Enter immediately at current price. Strong momentum confirmation.
MARKETConf ≥80% + Supertrend aligned + 4H trend alignedStrong HTF + LTF alignment. Enter now, don't wait for pullback.
LIMITDefault for most signalsWait for price pullback to entry zone (recent 5-bar swing ± ATR buffer).
⚠ RISK ENTRY2+ risk flags present OR conf <50% with 1 flagEnter at current price (limit pointless in risky conditions). Use wider SL (3×ATR).
SL Calculation
LONG SL = min(10-bar_swing_low − 0.2×ATR, entry − slMultiplier×ATR)
SHORT SL = max(10-bar_swing_high + 0.2×ATR, entry + slMultiplier×ATR)

slMultiplier = 2.5× (low vol) / 2.2× (medium) / 2.0× (high vol >3.5%)
Risk entry: 3.0× ATR always

Rationale: SL beyond structure + ATR buffer = reduces noise stops
TP Calculation
risk = |entry − SL|
TP1 = entry + 1.2× risk (RR ~1.2:1 — conservative, higher hit rate)
TP2 = entry + 2.5× risk (RR ~2.5:1 — full target)

After TP1 hit → SL moves to breakeven (entry price)
TP2 now risk-free: worst case = breakeven, best case = full profit

Trade Lifecycle
1
PENDING — Limit order placed. Waiting for price to reach entry zone. Expires if not filled within 7 days.
2
OPEN — Order filled (MARKET instant, LIMIT when price touches entry). SL and TP1/TP2 now active.
3
TP1 HIT — Price reached TP1. Close 50–70% of position here. SL automatically moves to breakeven (entry price). Remaining position is now risk-free.
4a
BREAKEVEN — After TP1, price pulls back to entry (BE SL hit). No loss — TP1 profits secured. Acceptable outcome.
4b
TP2 HIT — Full target reached. Maximum profit. Best outcome.
✗
SL HIT — Price hit stop loss. Loss = (entry − SL) / entry × 100%. With 2.5× ATR SL, typical loss = 2–4% of position.
